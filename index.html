Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? I've got answers

What is your design process?

My process involves five key steps: Discovery (understanding your brand), Research (market analysis), Concept (initial designs), Refine (revisions based on feedback), and Deliver (final assets). This ensures a strategic approach to every project.

How long does it take to design a logo?

Typically, initial concepts are delivered within 3-5 business days. The entire process, including revisions, usually takes 1-2 weeks depending on the package selected and feedback turnaround time.

How many revisions do I get?

Basic packages include 2 revisions, while Standard and Premium packages offer unlimited revisions. I work until you're 100% satisfied with the final result.

What file formats will I receive?

Basic packages include PNG and JPG files. Standard and Premium packages include vector files (AI, EPS, SVG) and PDF, ensuring you have everything needed for print and digital use.

How do you come up with business names?

I combine creative brainstorming with strategic research, considering your brand values, target audience, market positioning, and domain availability. Each suggestion is crafted to be memorable, pronounceable, and trademark-friendly.

Do you offer refunds?

Due to the creative nature of design work, refunds are not typically offered after work has begun. However, I offer unlimited revisions to ensure your satisfaction. If you're unsatisfied after the first concept presentation, we can discuss alternatives.
